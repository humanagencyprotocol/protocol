Why Projects Drift

Updates live across docs, chats, and personal AI agents that never talk. Everyone keeps moving, but not toward the same goal. By the time misalignment is obvious, deadlines and trust are already damaged.

The issue isn't effort—it's drift. Nearmydear makes alignment the default. It hears when humans or agents start pulling in different directions and nudges the right person to settle the ambiguity immediately.

How Nearmydear Works

1

Watch the flow

It listens to discussions, briefs, and AI-generated tasks without exporting private content.

2

Spot drift

When intent, tone, or priorities diverge, the system flags it instantly.

3

Ask the human

A single, well-timed question to the owner clears up the ambiguity.

4

Auto-sync everyone

Once answered, every teammate and agent continues with the same understanding.
